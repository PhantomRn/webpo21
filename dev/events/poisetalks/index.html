POISETalks

POISETalks is a series of talks that consist of five online seminars and one online workshop. This event will be held in six days. We provide a platform to give new perspectives for audiences with updated issues related to technical and non-technical aspects in industries that we hope can enhance professional youth in sustainability of industry and related sectors.
