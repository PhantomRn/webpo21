About

POISE UGM

Process Engineering Series of Events or POISE is an international annual event held by Keluarga Mahasiswa Teknik Kimia (KMTK) Universitas Gadjah Mada. POISE is a combination of previous events in KMTK, Safecom and PIT. Safecom was meant to build awareness about the importance of safety, while PIT was meant to raise issues in the world of petrochemical, geothermal, oil, and gas. POISE is a platform for future leaders to compete in developing creative and innovative ideas and hone critical thinking to answer sustainable problems.
