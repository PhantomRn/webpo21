CATALYST

Chemical Engineering Challenge
on Applied Science and Mathematics

CATALYST is a competition for high school students and consists of three types of challenge, i.e., Olympiad, practical work, and quiz contest. The competition aims to introduce chemical engineering through questions related to mathematics, physics, and chemistry.

Terms and Conditions

This competition is eligible for an active senior high school student during the series of CATALYST 2021 competition.

Each team can only register one email and it is recom-mended to use the leader’s email. It is obligated to use an active email because information will be distributed through email.

Each team consist of 2-3 high school student from the same school and is allowed from different year.

There is no limitation for each high school team. In other words, every school is allowed to send more than one team to join CATALYST 2021.

Each participant is bounded to one team only.

Any form of cheating in this competition will result in point reduction in the assessment, disqualification, or cancellation of the winner competition after being determined.

Further notices related to the CATALYST 2021 will be announced through the official website of POISE 2021 on poiseugm.net and POISE 2021’s social media accounts.

Each participant is limited to register for only one team.

Make sure the information is filled in correctly.
