Terms and Conditions

Participants must be registered as active students of Diploma or Undergraduate (S1).

Participants consist of individuals or teams (maximum of 2 students) from any majors from the same institution. Team members from different batches are allowed.

Each university is allowed to send more than 1 team.

Change of team member is prohibited.

Each participant is limited to register for only one team and one competition.

Each team must submit only one poster before September 24th, 2021 at 23.59 West Indonesian Time (GMT+7)
