Process Simulation Competition

PSiCo is one of competitions in POISE 2021 in the field of simulation process competition. In this competition, participants should give analysis and recommendation on the problem that happened in some unit in industry using process simulation software so that the industry can develop to be a more sustainable industry

PSICO Theme

Applicating Green Industry Culture in
The Energy Industry for Sustainability Purposes
